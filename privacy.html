Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

About This Policy

At VanillaGift ("we," "our," or "us"), safeguarding your personal data is a core priority. This document describes the ways we gather, handle, share, and secure your information whenever you access evanillamygift.com or use any of our gift card services.

Data We Gather

The following categories of data may be collected during your interactions with our platform:

  • Card Details: Your card number, expiry date, and security code submitted during balance lookups. These are handled instantly during the request and never retained on our infrastructure.
  • Personal Contact Data: Your name, email, and telephone number provided through our support channels or inquiry forms.
  • Behavioral and Technical Data: Information such as your browser version, device operating system, IP address, visited pages, and session duration, gathered automatically via cookies and comparable tracking methods.

Purpose of Data Usage

Your information serves the following specific functions within our operations:

  • Fulfilling balance lookup requests and presenting your current card value
  • Addressing and resolving your support tickets and questions
  • Enhancing the quality and performance of our website and offerings
  • Evaluating visitor behavior and identifying service improvement opportunities
  • Detecting and preventing fraud or any unauthorized access attempts

Protecting Your Data

We apply recognized security protocols to keep your personal information safe. Every transmission on our site is protected through SSL/TLS encryption. Once a balance inquiry has been completed, we do not retain your complete card number, security code, or any other sensitive payment card data.

Cookie Usage

We rely on cookies to deliver a smoother experience on our platform. These are small data files placed on your device that allow us to learn how visitors navigate our pages. You may adjust your cookie preferences within your browser settings, though doing so could limit some site functionality.

External Service Providers

Certain functions on our platform involve external partners, including analytics tools and payment processing vendors. Each of these providers operates under its own privacy terms and may independently collect user data. We never sell, lease, or trade your personal information to outside parties for promotional use.

What You Are Entitled To

As a user of our services, you may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Obtain a copy of the personal information we maintain about you
  • Ask us to correct any data that is outdated or inaccurate
  • Instruct us to erase your personal records from our systems
  • Withdraw from any promotional or marketing messages
  • File a formal concern with the relevant data protection regulator

Policy Revisions

This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updated versions will appear on this page along with a new effective date. We recommend checking back regularly to stay informed.

Get in Touch
