A Bit About Us

VanillaGift was built to take the guesswork out of gift card ownership. We help you look up balances, track spending, and activate Visa and Mastercard gift cards — all from one place. Millions of people across the U.S. already count on us.

We believe managing a prepaid gift card should feel effortless. It doesn't matter if someone gave it to you or you picked one up yourself — our goal is to make sure every dollar on that card works for you.

How It Works

Managing your gift cards here is quick and hassle-free. There's nothing to download, no app to install, and you won't need to create an account. Just punch in your card info and you'll see your balance and recent activity right away.

We handle Visa, Mastercard, and Discover prepaid gift cards from U.S. issuers. And because our site never sleeps, you can look up your card details whenever it's convenient — morning, noon, or midnight.
